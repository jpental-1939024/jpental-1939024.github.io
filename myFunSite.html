Introducing Jazzy's Website

Don't know how to spend your weekend nights? Well, you've come to the perfect place! My website will help you find out the perfect thing to do with your night according to what you love to do.

Take our survey, connect with others and let's get started!

About Us

The idea to create this website came to my when I found my friends and I constantly texting each other to figure out how to spend our free nights. We tried to brainstorm ideas that would work for us which helped. However, I am sure many people struggle to find something fun to do with their nights. So, I created this website to help people determine how to spend their nights, and the results are very personalized.
